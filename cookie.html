What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that websites place on your device—such as your computer, tablet, or smartphone—when you browse online. These files store limited information related to your activity on the site and are used to improve functionality, performance, personalization, and security. Cookies do not harm your device and cannot access any files or information stored on your computer.

Why We Use Cookies

At areamerchant, we use cookies to ensure our website functions effectively, to provide an optimized browsing experience, and to deliver content that aligns with your preferences. Cookies allow us to remember user settings, recognize returning visitors, collect analytical data, and serve relevant advertisements. Our goal is to offer an intuitive and seamless user journey tailored to your needs and interests.

Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize the cookies we use into several groups, each serving a specific purpose to enhance your interaction with our website:

1. Essential Cookies: These are required for the basic functionality of the website. They allow you to navigate pages, use secure areas, and perform actions like accessing your user account. Blocking these cookies may impact the core performance of the site.

2. Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us measure and analyze how visitors interact with the site—such as pages visited, time spent, bounce rate, and error messages. They do not collect personal information and are used solely to improve website functionality and content relevance.

3. Functional Cookies: These remember user preferences and previous interactions, such as language selection, region, or customized interface settings. By remembering your choices, they enhance usability and create a more personalized experience on return visits.

4. Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies track browsing behavior to deliver relevant advertising across other websites and social media platforms. They may also limit how often you see an ad and help measure the effectiveness of campaigns. These are often set by trusted third-party partners under contractual agreements.

5. Third-Party Cookies: We may allow third-party services to place cookies on our site to support features such as embedded videos, live chats, social media sharing, or integrated analytics. These cookies are governed by the privacy policies of the respective providers and may be used for independent tracking or profiling.

Your Cookie Choices and Controls

You are in control of how cookies are used on your device. Most browsers allow you to accept, reject, or delete cookies through their settings. You can typically manage your preferences in the privacy or security section of your browser menu. Note that disabling certain cookies may impact your ability to use some features of our site effectively.

For more control, you may use browser add-ons or extensions that block trackers, or adjust your privacy settings using global platforms such as the Global Privacy Control (GPC) or your browser’s “Do Not Track” (DNT) feature—where available and supported.
